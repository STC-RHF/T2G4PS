About Us

Who We Are

"Time To Give For Palestine" is a **non-profit organization** working to channel donations into humanitarian, educational, and healthcare projects in Palestine. We prioritize **transparency** and **collaboration** with local partners to ensure help reaches those in need.

We believe in community participation and in building **sustainable solutions** that empower families and local communities for the long run.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide **immediate aid** and long-term programs focusing on health, education, and relief for vulnerable communities.

Vision

**Thriving communities** with equal opportunities, supported by strong local institutions able to create change.

Our Impact

Over the years, we have supported thousands of families through **humanitarian relief**, education, healthcare, and **sustainable development programs** in Palestine.

Education

Built schools, supported thousands of students with scholarships, and provided essential learning resources.

Healthcare

Delivered medical aid, supported hospitals with equipment, and ran mobile clinics for underserved areas.

Relief Aid

Distributed food packages, clean water, and emergency kits to families in urgent need.

Sustainable Development

Launched community-based projects that empower families to build income and ensure long-term stability.

Key Achievements

  • ✅ Delivered food and medical aid to over **1.2 million individuals**.
  • ✅ Completed **85+ community development projects** across Palestine.
  • ✅ Built 30 schools and supported **15,000 students** with scholarships.
  • ✅ Partnered with international organizations for **healthcare initiatives**.
  • ✅ Provided **emergency relief** during times of crisis.
